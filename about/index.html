我的介绍

天空没有翅膀的痕迹，而我已飞过。

I leave not race of wings in the air,but I am glad I have had my flight

让生如夏花般绚烂，死如秋叶般静美。

Let life bebeautiful like summer flowers,and death like autumn leaves.
